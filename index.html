Welcome to KIKI HUB!!

KIKI HUB is a procedural product design tool for 3D printing.

There is no doubt that 3D printing is one of the greatest innovations of our time. It has transformed the complex journey of unique products, delivering them straight to your home using just a few megabytes of digital data. No shipping logistics, no warehouses, and zero packaging waste.

However, the real bottleneck has always been design flexibility and the steep learning curve of complex CAD software. This has created a massive wall that holds people back.

Today, we are introducing a brand-new tool to break down that wall. KIKI HUB brings together a variety of procedural product design tools that go beyond just uniform scaling. It allows you to design objects based on actual functionality and unlock endless creative possibilities—all effortlessly controlled through simple, intuitive sliders.

Everyone can download this tool and try it for free. We are confident you can create the perfect box or vase customized just for you, ready to print in less than 15 minutes.

Key Features

Perfect Auto-Tolerance

Flawless Snap-Fit, Zero Friction Out of the Box. Say goodbye to parts that are too tight to assemble or too loose to hold. KIKI HUB's advanced procedural geometry core automatically calculates the perfect mechanical tolerance required for structural connections. No matter how much you scale or resize your models, every joint, interlocking latch, and puzzle box edge will snap together seamlessly and securely on your first print minutes.

Zero CAD Skills Required

Ultra-Fast, Slider-Based Intuitive Modding. Forget the steep learning curve of complex CAD software like Fusion360 or Blender. Built on a standardized, highly scannable UI pattern, KIKI HUB allows anyone to customize 3D models instantly. Just grab your tape measure, input your real-world dimensions via sliders, and watch the model adapt in real-time. Ready to export in under 60 seconds.

Tailored to Real-World Spaces

1-Click Spatial Optimization & Smart Partitioning. Turn your 3D printer into a bespoke manufacturing factory for your home. With our innovative algorithms—like the one powering KIKI BOX—you simply enter your drawer or shelf dimensions, and the app instantly sub-divides the area into custom modular parts that fit your real-world spaces with 100% precision.

Clean Topology, Ready to Print

100% Manifold Guaranteed. No More Digital Waste. Unlike other web generators that spit out un-sliceable 'polygon hell' with overlapping faces and broken edge flows, KIKI HUB delivers pristine, mathematically sound 3D data. Co-created with generative logic, our meshes are guaranteed to be 100% manifold, lightweight, and optimized for fast slicing in Bambu Studio or Cura. Enjoy flawless surface finishes and zero failed prints.

100% Free for Everyone

Empowering the Global Maker Community without Limits. We believe high-end parametric tools shouldn't be locked behind expensive monthly subscriptions. KIKI HUB is completely free for makers, creators, and 3D printing enthusiasts worldwide. Access all smart home and lifestyle products, customize to your heart's content, and generate infinite variations—all with zero paywalls. Powered by creators, built for the community.
